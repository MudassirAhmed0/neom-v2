NEOM community members enjoy a wide range of sports, fitness and leisure activities, including fitness classes, sports coaching, cinema and much more. Recreational activities offer the access to the swimming pools, gyms, tennis courts, TV room, board game rooms, outdoor Recreation fields, and Cinema.

About KISWA Initiative

We are continually listening to feedback from residents about the improvements youd like to see in our NEOM communities. By popular demand, we are pleased to announce a new initiative that lies at the heart of NEOMs vision and values.

Through a collective effort by Community Services and Resource & Waste Management we are pleased to provide the community with the opportunity to donate unwanted clothing for re-use and recycling. Weve partnered with KISWA to give your unwanted clothing items a new lease of life. KISWA is Saudi organization which donates second-hand clothing to charities within the Kingdom. Good quality clothes are distributed in Saudi charities and the damaged clothes go to recycling facilities.

Clear Out To Help Out

We want to provide the NEOM community members with a convenient way of donating their used clothing items to help people in need as well as helping the environment. Look out for the Smart Donation Containers inside the NEOM communities, where you can drop off any unwanted clean clothes, shoes, handbags and backpacks. You can find the locations of the containers in each community.

The donation initiative will start in NEOM Community 1, NEOM Community 2 and OXAGONCommunity from Monday, April 11, 2022.

Six Simple Steps To Make A Big Difference

We want to provide the NEOM community members with a convenient way of donating their used clothing items to help people in need as well as helping the environment. Look out for the Smart Donation Containers inside the NEOM communities, where you can drop off any unwanted clean clothes, shoes, handbags and backpacks. You can find the locations of the containers in each community.

1

Sort the clean clothes, shoes or bags you wish to donate

2

Find the closest Smart Donation Container in your NEOM Community

3

Place your items in the correct compartment based on their category - clothes, shoes, bags

4

Quality donations are sent to Saudi charities, while damaged donations are sent to recycling facilities

5

Donations are sorted according to their quality

6

KISWA will collect on a weekly basis from each NEOM Community

We encourage you to protect the environment and help others by donating your unwanted clothes, shoes and bags.
