Terms And Conditions Of Requests During Occupancy

IMPORTANT Guidelines and Responsibilities for NEOM Residents:

This form must be completed by the NEOM resident and submitted to Community Services.

It is the responsibility of the NEOM Resident to ensure that this form is submitted for processing to Community Services before any modification is made in the Accommodation Unit.

Completed form and supporting documents, i.e. copies of the equipment’s manual, photos, drawings, etc. must be submitted to respective community housing office

Resident shall shoulder the cost of the charges that might be applicable

NEOM Residents must ensure the following:

A)

Not to proceed with any modification in their accommodation unit without the necessary approval.

B)

All NEOM provided items upon arrival shall remain in the same condition throughout the stay

C)

The resident shall ensure that no personal belonging and/or modification causes any disturbance or inconvenience to other NEOM residents.

D)

NEOM indoor equipment shall not be stored outside the unit. Damages shall be dealt with according to the Housing Agreement.

I have read and acknowledged the above guidelines and responsibilities
