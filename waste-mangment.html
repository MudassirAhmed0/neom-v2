WASTE MANAGEMENT

NEOM is designing and building the world's most sustainable cities and regions based on a Circular Economy. But what is Circular Economy? It is an economic system that aims to eliminate waste throughout an entire value chain, including manufacturing, production, and consumption. Its value comes in preserving raw materials and eradicating waste altogether. It is divided into the following steps, in this order of priority: Refuse, Reduce, Reuse, Recycle & Compost, and Recover. When it comes to waste management, we believe simple and logical solutions deliver rewarding benefits. NEOM is already converting waste materials into valuable resources.
